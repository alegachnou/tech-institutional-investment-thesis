# Tech Institutional Investment Thesis

## Objectives
- Analyze institutional investment patterns in leading technology companies, focusing on Apple (AAPL) and Microsoft (MSFT).
- Compare ownership structures, historical performance, and analyst sentiment.

## Methodology
1. Retrieve current stock metrics (price, market cap, P/E, sector, etc.).
2. Compile daily price data for 2024 (Jan 1 – Nov 30) to assess YTD performance.
3. Extract the latest annual income statement for Apple to evaluate profitability.
4. Gather top institutional holders for both companies and identify overlapping investors.
5. Summarize analyst recommendations for Apple over the past six months.
6. Summarize recent news headlines for Apple.

## Data Sources
- Yahoo Finance (stock metrics, historical prices, income statements, holders, recommendations, news).
- SEC 13F filings.
- Market data APIs.

## Repository Structure
- `aapl_analysis.md` – Apple stock info, price summary, income statement, holders, analyst recommendations, news.
- `msft_analysis.md` – Microsoft stock info, price summary, holders.
- `README.md` – This overview.
